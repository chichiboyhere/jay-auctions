About

Product Auctioning

Connecting sellers with buyers

Jays Auction is an online market place that brings prospective sellers and buyers together. You can list items for sale ranging from art works and artifacts, to household items, clothes and jewellery, electronics and gadgets and so on. Potential buyers then place a bid for the listed item. The seller can close the bid at anytime he/she deems fit. Whoever emerges as the highest bidder on a given item as at the time the bid is closed is then announced as the winner of the bid and they can pay for the item to claim it.

What are you waiting for? Sign up or login to start selling or buying that item of choice.
